The Best Dramas According To Hamdalah

My top 3 of all times

The Kings Daughter Su-baek-yhang

A historical drama depicting the life of Soo Baek-hyang, the daughter of King Muryeong of Baekje.
I love Korean dramas especially the ones with historical/traditional settings.

The Lion King

The Lion King is a 1994 American animated musical drama film produced by Walt Disney Feature Animation.
Lion king was released by Walt Disney Pictures.
It still remains one of my best cartoons.

Vagabond

A stuntman involved in a tragic airplane crash ends up discovering a national corruption scandal in the process.
It is a drama with an intriguing plot carried along with great suspenses,I love dramas with suspenses.
